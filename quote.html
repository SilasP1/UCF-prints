This calculator is an estimate only. Final pricing happens after file review so standard prints can stay affordable while larger or urgent jobs are reviewed more carefully.

How estimates are calculated

Subtotal = $5 setup fee + material cost + ($1.50 per estimated print hour). Estimated total reflects queue tier rules, minimum pricing, and custom-review conditions where needed.

  • Economy, 7-10 days: lowest cost with flexible queue placement
  • Standard, 3-5 days: 1.00x
  • Priority, 1-2 days: 1.35x
  • Rush / same-day: 1.75x
  • Immediate / emergency: custom quote only
