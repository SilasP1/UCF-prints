Pricing logic

Final pricing is based on production effort and delivery risk, not only part size.

  • Material
  • Print time
  • Filament used
  • Part complexity
  • Support material
  • Deadline urgency
  • Failed-print risk

Long-print policy

  • Prints over 12 hours require manual review.
  • Longer prints are not automatically rejected.
  • They are marked as Special Quote Required.
  • Same-day or immediate long prints require premium pricing.
