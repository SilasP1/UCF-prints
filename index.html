How to read this

TI Lab is usually the cheapest option if you have time, access, and want to manage the print yourself. Online print farms are useful for specialty materials or professional-grade jobs, but shipping makes them slower and more expensive for most student projects. UCF Prints is built for students who need a usable part quickly without dealing with the printing process.

UCF Prints fills the gap between doing it yourself at the TI Lab and waiting on an online print farm.
