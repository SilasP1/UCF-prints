Capacity rules

  • Default daily print capacity: 12 total print-hours.
  • Standard and Priority jobs should fit within the 12-hour limit by default.
  • Rush, same-day, immediate, or longer-than-12-hour jobs require manual approval and special pricing.
  • Long jobs are not auto-rejected and are marked Special Quote Required.
