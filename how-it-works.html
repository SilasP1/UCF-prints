Before you order

The practical policies

Quotes and payment

The calculator is an estimate. Final pricing follows file review. Payment is required after approval and before printing begins.

Timing and pickup

Standard jobs usually take 3–5 days. Pickup is scheduled near the Student Union. Rush and special meeting requests depend on availability.

Models and reprints

We review printability, but customers remain responsible for model dimensions and intended function. Reprint decisions depend on whether the print or the supplied model caused the problem.
